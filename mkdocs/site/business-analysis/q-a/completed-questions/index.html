    Completed Questions

    Q-001 (business_rules)

    • Question: What leave types does the system need to support, and which ones require advance notice — and how much notice?
    • Why Relevant:
    • Response: Annual Leave (14 days notice), Sick Leave (no notice required), Unpaid Leave (30 days notice), Maternity/Paternity Leave (30 days notice)
    • Queue:

    Q-002 (business_rules)

    • Question: Does the 'no overlapping leave' rule apply per employee only, or also across the team?
    • Why Relevant:
    • Response: Per employee only — overlapping requests for the same employee are blocked; team capacity is visible to managers but not enforced by the system
    • Queue:

    Q-003 (workflow_process)

    • Question: Is the approval chain single-level or multi-level?
    • Why Relevant:
    • Response: Single-level: employee submits to direct manager. HR Admin can override or intervene but is not part of the standard approval chain.
    • Queue:

    Q-004 (workflow_process)

    • Question: Can an employee modify or cancel a leave request that has already been approved? If so, does it require re-approval?
    • Why Relevant:
    • Response: Employees can cancel approved leave; cancellation triggers a notification to the manager and the balance is restored. Modification of approved leave requires re-approval.
    • Queue:

    Q-005 (stakeholders_roles)

    • Question: What are the notification triggers and channels?
    • Why Relevant:
    • Response: Email notifications only. Submission: notify manager. Approval/rejection: notify employee. Cancellation: notify manager. Modification: notify manager if pending, notify manager and HR Admin if approved.
